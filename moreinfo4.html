    Hydraulic Press (10 TON)

    A 10-ton hydraulic press is a powerful and versatile tool used for applying compressive force to various materials, achieving tasks that require significant pressure. This type of press operates on the principle of hydraulic fluid dynamics, where an incompressible liquid (usually oil) is used to generate force through pistons within a sealed system. By applying force to the smaller piston, the liquid transmits this pressure to a larger piston, amplifying the output force in accordance with Pascal's law.
